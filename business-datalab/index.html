About Us

Business Data Laboratory is a consulting firm with a focus on delivering solution that enhance business performance by harnessing the values from business-related data. Our collaborative approach to service delivery, enabling us to work closely with our clients to deliver solutions the add value and growth to your business.

Services

At Business Data Laboratory, our collaborative approach to service delivery, enabling us to work closely with our clients to deliver solutions the add value and growth to their business.

Data Science

Our data science team work with clients to deliver machine learning models tailored to their business through provision of strategic guidance all through the model development, training, and deployment. We also provide model maintenance and support on data literacy

Shiny Development

Business Data Laboratory develop Shiny applications that are easy-to-use, scalable, and secure for small to enterprise clients. Our team comprise of passionate and experienced R and Shiny developers with added experience working with web technologies to create a value-based solution for your business.

Data Visualization and Reporting

We help clients discover and tell story that lives in your data using the most modern tools for data visualization and reporting. We create custom visualization and report branded specially for your project and organization.

Business Analysis/Product Owner Consultancy

We provide business analysis and Product Owner services allows us to help manage the challenges associated with refining client’s vision and maximizing the value of their product to meet both consumer and stakeholder expectations.

Research and Training

Our research services are tailored at helping your business achieve success by harnessing the values from your data. From data sourcing, analysis, visualization, reporting, and modeling. You can achieve more through your data. We also have a unique and career-tailored curriculum specific for you as an organization or professional. These courses are trained by passionate, certified and experienced trainers.

Meetups and Webinars

As an organization committed to giving back to it's community, we organize webinars that help showcase our various products, technologies we use and also career guidance. We aslo support local communities like Abuja R User Group and R-Ladies Abuja.
